For the library card application, there are two ways you can choose either:

a) Bring your passport photo and identification card (IC) to the library. Fill out an application form at our receptionist counter and you will get your card at the same day within few hours.

OR

b) Fill out the application form of library card at our website (www.TheSunsetLibrary.com.my). You also have to upload your passport photo there. Once your card is ready, we will inform you via email and phone for you to collect the card at our library.
