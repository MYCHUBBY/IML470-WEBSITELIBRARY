ABOUT US

Our Mission: To foster a love for reading and learning by providing free access to a wide range of informational, educational, and recreational materials.

Our Vision: To be a vibrant community hub that supports and inspires lifelong learning, creativity, and innovation.

History of sunset library

The Early Years

The Sunset Library was founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated community members who saw the need for a public space where people of all ages could access books and educational resources. Initially housed in a small storefront, the library quickly became a beloved fixture in the neighborhood

Expansion and Growth

By the 2013s, the library's collection had outgrown its original location. Thanks to generous donations from local businesses and a successful community fundraising campaign, a new, larger building was constructed in 2014. This new facility allowed the library to expand its services, including the introduction of children's reading programs and adult education classes.

Embracing Technology

In the 2018s and 2019s, the Sunset Library embraced the digital revolution. The library added computers for public use, launched its first electronic catalog, and began offering digital resources such as e-books and online databases. These advancements made it easier for patrons to access a wealth of information from around the world.

Renovation and Modernization

Recognizing the need to stay current with the evolving needs of the community, the library underwent a major renovation in 2020. The updated facility featured modern amenities, including a state-of-the-art multimedia room, study spaces, and a community meeting room. These improvements have allowed the library to host a variety of events and workshops, further solidifying its role as a community hub.

Today& Beyond

Today, the Sunset Library continues to be a vital resource for the community, offering a diverse collection of books, multimedia resources, and a wide range of programs for all ages. As we look to the future, we remain committed to adapting and growing to meet the changing needs of our patrons. Our vision is to be a welcoming and inclusive space where everyone can explore, learn, and connect.
